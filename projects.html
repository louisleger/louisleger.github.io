Unsupervised Connectome Learning in fMRI State Mixtures
Gaussian Mixture Models are universal in their ability to approximate continuous densities. We show the inferred state connectomes of the spinal cord with the unsupervised Graph Laplacian Mixture Model. We find laplacians highly correlated with prior knowledge despite a poor signal and show the limits of spinal cord fMRI by parametrizing the feasibility of unsupervised clustering with multivariate Gaussians.
